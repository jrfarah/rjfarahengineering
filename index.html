Rachid Farah, PE, NCEES, President

Mr. Farah is a licensed structural engineer registered in over 15 states. He has over 30 years of experience in structural engineering design and construction administration. Prior to forming RJFE, Mr. Farah worked with leading national and international firms in varying capacities from project engineer to firm principal. His work experience encompasses steel, concrete, masonry, wood as well as shallow and deep foundation. His projects included environmental structures, mixed use, retail, hospitality, institutional and residential. Mr. Farah is a past President of the Boston Association of Structural Engineers (BASE).
